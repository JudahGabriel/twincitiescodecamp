Twin Cities Code Camp Sponsorship

Twin Cities Code Camp is the largest free developer event in Minnesota. Your sponsorship provides a cost-effective way for your organization to engage the Twin Cities technical community, reach out to local professionals, and possibly find new team members for your organization.

Support from sponsoring organizations is critical to the viability of Code Camp. Support is needed to help with the following Code Camp activities:

  • Venue and facility fees
  • Giveaways for the attendees
  • Speaker dinner event on Saturday night

Several sponsorship levels are available to suit your organization and donating couldn't be easier! We accept donations by credit card (through PayPal) or by check. More details about payments are below, otherwise feel free to contact us at sponsors@twincitiescodecamp.com - we are more than happy to work with you.

Sponsorship Levels

The following sponsorship levels are available with their associated benefits:

Platinum - $2000

  • A linked banner on our web site indicating Platinum sponsorship level.
  • Space for your own information/marketing table during the day at the event.
  • At the opening ceremony your organization will be recognized as a Platinum Sponsor.
  • Two reserved session spaces (speakers must submit their information before the CFS deadline and indicate that they are affiliated with your company)
  • Attend speaker happy-hour event on Saturday (2 representatives maximum)
  • Your flyer or information available as a handout to attendees.

Gold - $1,000

  • A linked banner on our web site indicating Gold sponsorship level.
  • At the opening ceremony your organization will be recognized as a Gold Sponsor.
  • One reserved session space (speaker must submit their information before the CFS deadline and indicate that they are affiliated with your company)
  • Your flyer or information available as a handout to attendees.

Silver - $500

  • A linked banner on our web site indicating Silver sponsorship level.
  • At the opening ceremony your organization will be recognized as a Silver Sponsor.
  • Your flyer or information available as a handout to attendees.

Bronze - Value of material donated

  • Typically this involves contribution of product, magazines, books, etc.
  • Logo on website
  • Your flyer or information available as a handout to attendees.
